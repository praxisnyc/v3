23 July 2019 @ 10PM
REBAR

$15 (includes 2 free drinks)

Bored on a Tuesday?
Need to recharge your creative mind?
Looking to meet local creatives, network and exchange ideas?

Well, never fear! Richard Burrowes Drink & Draw is here!

Every Tuesday at Rebar Chelsea hosts a social drawing event with focus on figure drawing from a live model.

Model poses can range from 2 minutes to 20 minutes.

No need to be a pro, stick figures and hand-turkeys welcome! Bring your friends or enjoy an evening of creative exploration and meeting local artists. Each event will have a new models, and plenty to drink!

Some materials are on hand or you can bring your own!

29 December 2018 @ 10PM
Tabla Rasa

Text or call 917-880-8337
Uninstructed
Figure drawing (nude) sessions alternating Mondays 6:30-9:00pm with breaks every 20 minutes. Open sessions $15. Easels, chairs, and taborets available. No solvents or volatile media requiring ventilation. The atmosphere is comfortable, clean, and congenial. We have directional lighting, snacks and fresh brewed decaf coffee. From Manhattan, D or N train to 36th St., cross platform for one local stop on R to 45 Street. Street parking is plentiful. Text or call for more information and/or to join email list for date confirmations. Sessions with private or semi-private instruction can be arranged. We are also occasionally seeking models and would appreciate contact.
